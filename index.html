About Hisani

At Hisani, we're passionate about bringing the authentic flavors and traditions of Japan to your table. Our expert chefs use only the finest ingredients to create an ever-evolving menu that will transport your senses to the heart of Japan.

Menu

Omakase

A unique and personalized Japanese dining experience curated by expert chefs.

Sushi

A Japanese dish made of vinegar rice, seafood, and vegetables, served fresh or seasoned.

Nabe

A Japanese hot pot dish made with various ingredients, cooked and served in a communal pot.

Sashimi

A Japanese dish of raw fish or seafood, expertly sliced and served with soy sauce.

Sake

A Japanese alcoholic beverage made from fermented rice, with a smooth and distinctive flavor.

Drinks
